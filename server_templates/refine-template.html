Range Map Refinement Habitat Change Analysis
Expert range maps delineate the outer boundaries of where a species is expected to occur, but overestimate species’ actual distribution. This tool allows combining habitat suitability knowledge with remote-sensing based environmental layers to improve the precision of our species range estimate. Where available, a base refinement with current expert suitability information is provided.
