Range Map Refinement Habitat Change Analysis
Species range maps are expert estimations of where a species can potentially live. However the area within a given range encompasses a wide variety of habitats, and the actual area inhabited by the species may in fact be much smaller. This tool allows expert range maps to be refined by known species habitat and elevation preferences.
